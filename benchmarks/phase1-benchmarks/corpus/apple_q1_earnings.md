# Apple Q1 2024 Earnings Report

## Financial Highlights
Apple today announced financial results for its fiscal 2024 first quarter ended December 30, 2023. The Company posted quarterly revenue of $119.6 billion, up 2 percent year over year, and quarterly earnings per diluted share of $2.18, up 16 percent year over year.

“Today Apple is reporting revenue growth for the December quarter fueled by iPhone sales, and an all-time revenue record in Services,” said Tim Cook, Apple’s CEO.

## Segment Performance
*   **iPhone**: Revenue was $69.70 billion, up 6% from last year.
*   **Mac**: Revenue was $7.78 billion, up 1% from last year.
*   **iPad**: Revenue was $7.02 billion, down 25% from last year.
*   **Wearables, Home and Accessories**: Revenue was $11.95 billion, down 11% from last year.
*   **Services**: Revenue was $23.12 billion, up 11% from last year.

## Forward Outlook
For the March quarter, Apple expects company total revenue and iPhone revenue to be similar to the prior year.
