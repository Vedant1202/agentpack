Method of Determining Segment Income or Loss
Management evaluates the performance of our operating segments separately to individually monitor the different factors affecting financial performance. Our Company
manages income taxes from continuing operations and certain treasury-related items, such as interest income and expense, on a global basis within the Corporate operating
segment. We evaluate segment performance based on income or loss from continuing operations before income taxes.
