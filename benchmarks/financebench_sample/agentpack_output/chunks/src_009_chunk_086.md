Credit Risk
54% of domestic plan assets are invested in bonds with an average credit rating of AA-. These bonds are subject to both credit and default risk and changes in the risk
could lead to a decline in the value of these bonds. 
Liquidity Risk
1% of the domestic securities are invested in Level 3 securities. These are long-term investments in private equity and private real estate investments that may not
mature or be sellable in the near-term without significant loss.
As of  December 31, 2022 and 2021, the amount of Corning common stock included in equity securities was not significant.
