structure in support of our strategy. Charges incurred in our International segment primarily related to our decision to exit our operations in Mexico. All of our 
former stores in Mexico were closed as of the end of the first quarter of fiscal 2022. Charges incurred in our Domestic segment primarily related to actions taken 
to align our organizational structure in support of our strategy. We do not expect to incur material future restructuring charges related to this initiative and no 
material liability remains as of January 28, 2023.
