Dividends
On February 6, 2020, our Board of Directors declared a cash dividend of $0.41 per common share, payable on May 6, 2020, to shareholders of record at the close
of business on April 15, 2020.
On February 12, 2019, our Board of Directors declared a cash dividend of $0.37 per common share. On May 9, 2019, we made an aggregate cash dividend
payment of $283 million to shareholders of record at the close of business on March 28, 2019.
Capital Expenditures
We made capital expenditures of $116 million in 2019, as compared to $131 million in 2018. In 2020, we anticipate total capital expenditures of approximately
$135 million, primarily for computer hardware, leasehold improvements, and software purchases.
