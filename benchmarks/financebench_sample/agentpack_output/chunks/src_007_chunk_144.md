3.2    Circumstances in Which Benefits are Not Payable.
Notwithstanding any other provision in this Plan to the contrary, an employee is not entitled to benefits under this Plan if the
employee:
(a)    voluntarily terminates employment,
(b)    was Disabled or on a leave of absence (except for an Approved Leave of Absence) immediately prior to his or her termination of
employment,
(c)    prior to receiving any benefit under the Plan, is offered a Comparable Position, as determined by the Administrator, with the Company or one
of its Affiliates,
