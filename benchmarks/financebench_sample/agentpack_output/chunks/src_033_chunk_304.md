Preemptive and Conversion Rights
The Listed Preferred Stock is not subject to any preemptive rights and is not convertible into property or shares of any other class or 
series of our capital stock. 
Depositary, Transfer Agent, and Registrar
Computershare Inc. is the depositary, transfer agent and registrar for each series of the Listed Preferred Stock and the related 
depositary shares.
Voting Rights
Except as indicated below or except as expressly required by applicable law, the holders of the Listed Preferred Stock are not entitled 
to vote. Each share of Listed Preferred Stock a series is entitled to one vote on matters on which holders of that series are entitled to 
vote. The voting power of each series of Listed Preferred Stock depends on the number of shares in that series, and not on the 
aggregate liquidation preference or initial offering price of the shares of that series.
