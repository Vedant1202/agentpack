Death
If you die while you are eligible to vest in RSUs under this award, the RSUs will immediately vest and will be distributed in shares of 
Common Stock (after applicable tax withholding) to your designated beneficiary on file with the Firm’s Stock Administration 
Department, or if no beneficiary has been designated or survives you or if beneficiary designation is not recognized locally, then to 
your estate unless otherwise required by local legislation.  Any shares will be distributed no later than the end of the calendar year 
immediately following the calendar year which contains your date of death; however, our administrative practice is to register such 
shares in the name of your beneficiary or estate within 60 days of the Firm’s receipt of any required documentation.
