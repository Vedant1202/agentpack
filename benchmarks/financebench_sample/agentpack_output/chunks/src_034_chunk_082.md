Spirit AeroSystems
At December  31,  2017 ,  Other  assets  included $137 of  receivables  related  to  indemnifications  from  Spirit  AeroSystems,  Inc.  (Spirit)  for  costs
incurred related to pension and retiree medical obligations of former Boeing employees who were subsequently employed by Spirit. On July 12,
2018,  the  Delaware  Supreme  Court  denied  Boeing’s  claim  for  indemnification.  As  a  result,  in  the  second  quarter  we  wrote  off  the  receivables
attributable to the Spirit indemnifications.
