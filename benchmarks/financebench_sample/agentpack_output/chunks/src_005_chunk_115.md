REITs—REITs are invested in commingled funds. Commingled funds are valued to reflect the plan fund’s interest in the fund based on the reported year-
end net asset value. Since the net asset value is not directly observable for the commingled funds, they are categorized as Level 2.
Guaranteed annuity contracts—Guaranteed annuity contracts are categorized as Level 3 because the investments are not publicly quoted. Since these market
values are determined by the provider, they are not highly observable and have been categorized as Level 3. Exchange-traded future and option positions are
reported in accordance with changes in variation margins that are settled daily.
