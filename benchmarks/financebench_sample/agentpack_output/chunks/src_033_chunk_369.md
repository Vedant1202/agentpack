If, after the calculation of the Performance Ranking, there is a tie, the tie shall be disregarded for purposes of determining the Award

“Recognized Service” means the period of service as an employee set forth in the Firm’s applicable service-related policies.
“ROTCE” means for the Firm and each of the Performance Companies a percentage derived by, for each year in the Performance 
Period, dividing (i) annual earnings from continuing operations less dividends on preferred stock as set forth in published financial 
disclosures by (ii) the Average Tangible Common Equity for the year.  If, prior to the end of the vesting period, the Firm or any 
Performance Company restates its published financial statements for any year in the Performance Period, ROTCE for that year shall 
be recalculated for the Firm or Performance Company with the Performance Ranking adjusted, if necessary.  This calculation is used 
solely for purposes of the Performance Ranking.  
“Target Award Number” means the number of PSUs designated as such in the Award Agreement.
