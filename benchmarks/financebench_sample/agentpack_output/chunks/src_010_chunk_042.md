Retail Channel Net Revenues
The decrease in net revenues from retail channels for 2019, as compared to 2018, was primarily due to:
•
lower revenues recognized from Call of Duty: Black Ops 4, which was released in October 2018, as compared to Call of Duty: WWII, which was released
in November 2017;
•
lower revenues recognized from the Destiny franchise; and
•
lower revenues from Crash Bandicoot™ N. Sane Trilogy, which was released on the Xbox One, PC, and Nintendo Switch in June 2018.
The decrease was partially offset by:
•
revenues recognized from Crash Team Racing Nitro-Fueled, which was released in June 2019;
•
revenues from Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, which was released in March 2019; and
•
higher revenues recognized from Call of Duty: Modern Warfare, which was released in October 2019, as compared to Call of Duty: Black Ops 4.
