Service due to death prior to the satisfaction of the vesting conditions set forth in the Vesting Schedule of the Agreement, any portion of
the French-Qualified Option that has not vested as of such date will immediately vest and Participant’s rights under the French-Qualified

unexercised portion of the French-Qualified Option within six months of the date of death, the unexercised portion of the French-Qualified
