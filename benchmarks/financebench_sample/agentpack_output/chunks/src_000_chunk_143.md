Each series of preferred stock has a liquidation value and redemption price per share of $10,000, plus accrued but unpaid 
dividends. The aggregate liquidation value was $31.9 billion at March 31, 2021.
Redemptions
On April 30, 2021, the Firm announced that it will redeem on June 1, 2021 all $1.4 billion of its outstanding 6.10% non-
cumulative preferred stock, Series AA and all $1.2 billion of its outstanding 6.15% non-cumulative preferred stock, Series BB.
On March 1, 2020, the Firm redeemed all $1.43 billion of its 6.125% non-cumulative preferred stock, Series Y.
