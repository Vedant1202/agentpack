D.    Normal Retirement Income. Effective for any Local 51 Participant retiring on and after July 1, 2004, except as modified
by the provisions of Section 6.1(a) of the Plan, the benefit payable to a Local 51 Participant commencing on their Normal

1, 2012, their benefit shall be a monthly amount equal to the sum of:

    b)    $1 for each $144 of their Required Contribution Account, if any.
    (ii)    Terminated or Retired on or after July 1, 2012. For any Local 51 Participant who continues to be actively employed

    (c)    $1 for each $144 of their Required Contribution Account, if any.
