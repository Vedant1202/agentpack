“Firmwide Financial Threshold” means a cumulative return on tangible common equity for calendar years 2023, 2024 and 2025 of 
not less than 15%.  Cumulative return on tangible common equity means (i) the sum of the Firm’s reported net income for all three 
calendar years, divided by (ii) reported year-end tangible equity averaged over the three years.
“Government Office” means (i) a full-time position in an elected or appointed office in local, state, or federal government (including 
equivalent positions outside the U.S. or in a supranational organization), not reasonably anticipated to be a full-career position, or (ii) 
conducting a bona fide full-time campaign for such an elective public office after formally filing for candidacy, where it is customary 
and reasonably necessary to campaign full-time for the office.
