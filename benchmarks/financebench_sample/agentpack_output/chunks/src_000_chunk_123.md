Active and suspended foreclosure
At March 31, 2021 and December 31, 2020, the Firm 
had residential real estate loans, excluding those insured 
by U.S. government agencies, with a carrying value of 
$802 million and $846 million, respectively, that were 
not included in REO, but were in the process of active or 
suspended foreclosure.
In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, the Firm has 
temporarily suspended certain foreclosure activities. This 
could delay recognition of foreclosed properties until the 
foreclosure moratoriums are lifted.
