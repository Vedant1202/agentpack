EQUITY INSTRUMENTS 
 
Equity price movements affect our compensation expense as certain investments made by our employees in our deferred compensation 
plan are revalued. We use equity swaps to manage this risk. As of May 31, 2020, the net notional amount of our equity swaps was 
$146.9 million. These swap contracts mature in fiscal 2021.
