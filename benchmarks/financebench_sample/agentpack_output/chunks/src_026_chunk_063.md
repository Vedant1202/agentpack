2020 Restructuring Actions:
Divestiture-Related Restructuring

certain restructuring actions addressing corporate functional costs and manufacturing footprint across 3M in relation to the magnitude of amounts previously allocated/burdened
to the divested business. These actions affected approximately 1,300 positions worldwide and resulted in a second quarter 2020 pre-tax charge of $55 million, within Corporate
and Unallocated. The divestiture-related restructuring actions were recorded in the income statement as follows:
