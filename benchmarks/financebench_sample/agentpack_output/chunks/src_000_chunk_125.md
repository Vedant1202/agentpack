Credit card loan portfolio

originated and purchased by the Firm. Delinquency rates 
are the primary credit quality indicator for credit card 
loans. 
Refer to Note 12 of JPMorgan Chase's 2020 Form 10-K for 
further information on the credit card loan portfolio, 
including credit quality indicators.
