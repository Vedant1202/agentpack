HTM securities – credit risk
Credit quality indicator
The primary credit quality indicator for HTM securities is the 
risk rating assigned to each security. At March 31, 2021 
and December 31, 2020, all HTM securities were rated 
investment grade and were current and accruing, with 
approximately 97% and 98% rated at least AA+, 
respectively.
Allowance for credit losses
The allowance for credit losses on HTM securities was $94 
million and $19 million as of March 31, 2021 and 2020, 
respectively.
