Employee Stock Purchase Plan
The Company maintains a nonqualified employee stock purchase plan (the “ESPP”) that expires in 2027 through which employee participants (which
excludes certain of the Company’s executives) may use payroll deductions to acquire Company common stock at a purchase price of 85% of the fair market
value of the common stock at the end of a three-month purchase period. A total of 2.0 million shares may be issued under the ESPP, and as of December 31,
2022, there were 1.5 million shares of common stock reserved for issuance under the ESPP. The ESPP is considered compensatory. During the years ended
December 31, 2022, 2021 and 2020, the Company issued approximately 82,000, 80,000 and 86,000 shares, respectively, under the ESPP.
