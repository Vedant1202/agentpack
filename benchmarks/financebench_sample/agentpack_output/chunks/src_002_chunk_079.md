        The Company has access to a single, multi-tranche syndicated facility with a group of counterparty banks. The funding arrangements provide for $4.2 billion of facilities consisting of a 3 year
term loan tranche expiring in 2022, as well as 3, 4 and 5 year revolver tranches expiring between 2022 and 2024, which may be used to support our commercial paper borrowings. The agreements
include customary terms and conditions for a syndicated facility of this nature and the revolving tranches have two 12 month options available to management to extend the maturity date.
        On September 25, 2019 and December 15, 2019, the Company canceled $250.0 million and $100.0 million, respectively, of the $750.0 million term loan facility.
