Performance-Based Share Awards
Performance-based share awards generally vest upon the achievement of company performance goals based upon compound annual growth in enterprise 
revenue (“CAGR”) or attainment of net earnings (“adjusted net earnings”). The number of shares of common stock that could be distributed at the end of the 
three-year CAGR-incentive period may range from 0% to 150% of each share granted (“target”). Shares are granted at 100% of target. Awards based on 
adjusted net earnings vest 33% on each of the three annual anniversary dates following the grant date if the adjusted net earnings goal has been met.
