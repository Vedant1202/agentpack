2017 Exit Activities:
During the first quarter of 2017, the Company recorded net pre-tax charges of $24 million related to exit activities. These charges related to employee
reductions, primarily in Western Europe. During the fourth quarter of 2017, the Company recorded net pre-tax charges of $23 million related to exit
activities. These charges related to employee reductions, primarily in the United States and Western Europe.
