The main provisions of this standard eliminate the receivables
accounting guidance for troubled debt restructurings (“TDRs”) by
creditors while enhancing disclosure requirements when a borrower is
experiencing financial difficulty. Entities must apply the loan
refinancing and restructuring guidance for receivables to determine
whether a modification results in a new loan or a continuation of an
existing loan. Additionally, the amendments in this update require that
an entity disclose current-period gross write-offs by year of origination
for financing receivables and net investment in leases.
