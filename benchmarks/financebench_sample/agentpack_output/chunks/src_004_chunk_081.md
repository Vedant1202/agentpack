Revenues
•
Total revenues increased $16.2 billion, or 10.6%, to $169.2 billion in 2022 compared to 2021. The increase was primarily driven by increased pharmacy
claims volume, growth in specialty pharmacy and brand inflation, partially offset by continued client price improvements.
