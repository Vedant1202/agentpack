Restricted funds—The Company’s restricted funds primarily represent proceeds received from financings for the construction and capital improvement of facilities and from customers for future
services under operation, maintenance and repair projects.
Rabbi trust investments—The  Company’s rabbi trust investments consist of equity and index funds from which supplemental executive  retirement plan benefits and deferred  compensation
obligations can be paid. The Company includes these assets in other long-term assets on the Consolidated Balance Sheets.
Deposits—Deposits include escrow funds and certain other deposits held in trust. The Company includes cash deposits in other current assets on the Consolidated Balance Sheets.
