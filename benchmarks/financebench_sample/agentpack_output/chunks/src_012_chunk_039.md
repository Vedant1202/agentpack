outlined above and to finance our capital expenditure and payments for acquisitions that may be completed. We expect to continue to fund our

We decide on discretionary growth capital expenditures and acquisitions individually based on, among other factors, the return on investment
after related financing costs and the payback period of required upfront cash investments in light of our mid-term liquidity planning covering a
period of four years post the current fiscal year. Our long-term access to liquidity depends on both our results of operations and on the availability
of funding in financial markets.
