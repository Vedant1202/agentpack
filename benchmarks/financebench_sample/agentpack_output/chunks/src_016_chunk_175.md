19. SHARE-BASED COMPENSATION
RESTRICTED STOCK
Restricted Stock Units — The Company issues RSUs under its long-term compensation plan. The RSUs are generally granted based
upon a percentage of the participant's base salary. Most RSUs have a three-year vesting period and vest evenly in annual increments over that
period. In all circumstances, RSUs granted by AES do not entitle the holder the right, or obligate AES, to settle the RSU in cash or other assets
of AES.
